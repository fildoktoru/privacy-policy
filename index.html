Privacy Policy (English) 1.Data Collected and Purposes of Use Microphone Data: Our application uses your device’s microphone to provide voice command and speech recognition features. The recorded voice data is processed temporarily to analyze your pronunciation performance. This voice data is not stored long-term, sent to a server, or shared with third parties. Performance and Usage Data: Your progress, scores, stage results, and performance records (e.g., “PerformanceRecord”) within the application are stored locally on your device (using SharedPreferences). These data are used solely to personalize your app experience and to track your progress. The data remain on your device and are not transmitted to servers. Payment Transactions: Premium membership transactions are carried out using the Google Play Billing Library. During payment transactions, any sensitive user data is securely processed via Google Play. 2.Data Storage and Security All data is stored locally on your device. The application uses Android’s standard security measures to protect user data. 3.Sharing with Third Parties No collected data is shared with third parties without the user's consent. Microphone and performance data are used solely for in-app analysis purposes. 4.User Rights Since your application data is stored on your device, you can remove your data by deleting it or uninstalling the application. 5.Changes This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through the application.
